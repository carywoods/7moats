# Copy Notes

## Public-facing tone adjustment

Do **not** use the phrase “We do what we do for all humankind” as public-facing copy on the landing page or launch materials.

That phrase can remain an internal north star, but it reads too grandly for first-contact readers and may reduce credibility on a public landing page.

## Preferred public-facing substitutes

Use grounded language such as:

- Human success in the age of intelligent machines.
- A framework for what remains valuable when machines become increasingly capable.
- A practical framework for people, teams, and institutions navigating the AI era.
- Built for readers, educators, leaders, and citizens trying to understand what comes next.

## Landing page update needed

Replace the About-section sentence:

> Guiding phrase: We do what we do for all humankind.

With a lower-friction public line, such as:

> The work is guided by a simple question: what helps human beings live, work, build, and make meaning in a world of increasingly capable machines?
